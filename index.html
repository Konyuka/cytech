About Cytech Contractors

Cytech Contractors Ltd was established to provide unique, durable, quality, and affordable construction services. Our team of specialized individuals works together in a unified loop to render high demand services in the country. â€‹

Our services are divided into projects and maintenance works. Under projects, we do design, drawing, new construction, and project management for Residential units, Commercial spaces, Petrol Stations, Road Works, Institutions, and Sewage systems.

On the other hand, maintenance/specialized services entail waterproofing, interceptor cleaning and construction, biodigester services, painting, plumbing, tiling, and external works. In our goal to achieve the highest level of quality work, we conduct business in a lawful and transparent manner. We are duly registered by the National Construction Authority (NCA) under Building works, Road works, and Mechanical Engineering services.

Our teams work in what we call unity loop. This system enables us to work using specialized teams at every stage of the project. For instance, once the plaster is complete, our tiling team and painting team take over the site and then hands it over the finishing team which conducts a thorough inspection and final touches before handing over to the client. Each team has a team leader who is in charge of coordinating that specific task from start to completion. We like the unity loop system because it ensures that tasks are handled by specialized individuals in that particular sphere. We do not experiment with quality, we deliver it.
